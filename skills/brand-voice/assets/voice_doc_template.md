## Tone of Voice Dimensions

Positioned on the four dimensions of tone of voice (Nielsen Norman Group). Each row is its own spectrum: the score is read against **that row's poles** — **1 = the left-hand label, 5 = the right-hand label, 3 = balanced**. So on "Funny ↔ Serious", 1 is most funny and 5 is most serious.

> 1 = strongly the left-hand label · 2 = leans left · 3 = balanced · 4 = leans right · 5 = strongly the right-hand label

The **Position** column names the chosen pole in plain words and is the source of truth; the number is a convenience that must agree with it. A near-pole score is fine — many brands genuinely sit at an extreme (e.g. strongly respectful), so don't nudge a dimension toward the middle just to look balanced.
